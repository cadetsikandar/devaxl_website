The challenge

A platform too slow to grow on.

Northwind's billing dashboard had been bolted onto for six years. Page loads crept past four seconds, deploys were risky, and the team had stopped shipping features because every change risked breaking something else.

They needed the platform faster and safer to change — but couldn't afford downtime or a rewrite that paused the roadmap for a year.

What we built

An incremental, zero-downtime rebuild.

We embedded a senior squad alongside Northwind's team and modernized the platform in slices — replacing the rendering layer, introducing server components, and moving heavy queries behind a typed data layer.

Each slice shipped behind a flag and was measured in production before the next began, so the product kept improving while the roadmap kept moving.

The outcome

Faster, stable, and ready to build on.
